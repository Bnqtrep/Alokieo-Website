What is Alokieo?

Just Students Having Fun With AI

Alokieo started as a crazy idea: "What if we could build our own AI model?" We're just middle school and high school students who got tired of just using AI - we wanted to understand how it actually works!

This isn't some fancy startup. It's a learning project where we code, experiment, and occasionally break things (then fix them!). We're building Amber LLM to understand the magic behind ChatGPT and other AI models.
