What is Amber?

This is an LLM that can generate sentences. I hope this can work.

We started a insane idea: "What if we could build our own AI model?" We're just middle school and high school students who got tired of just using AI - we wanted to understand how it actually works!

This is an opportunity when we can experience how does an LLM work? We can experience what is it like to be to develop a project together!

Meet Amber - Our LLM

Why Build Another AI Model?

We're building Amber because we want to understand what does AI work. How do transformers work? What's attention mechanism? Instead of just reading about it, we're coding it ourselves!

I called it amber for some personal reasons.
